Moran Coefficient of the Spatial Filter

Description

Computes the Moran coefficient of the spatial filter.

Usage

MI.sf(gamma, evMI)

Arguments

gamma

vector of regression coefficients associated with the eigenvectors
evMI

Moran coefficient of eigenvectors

Value

Moran coefficient of the spatial filter.
