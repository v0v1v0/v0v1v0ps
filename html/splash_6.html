density_h2o R Documentation

Calculate density of water at 1 atm, g/cm^3

Description

This function calculates the temperature and pressure dependent density of pure water.

Usage

density_h2o(tc, pa)

Arguments

tc

double, air temperature, degrees C.
pa

double, atm pressure, Pa.

Value

double, kg/m^3.

References

Chen, C.T., Fine, R.A. and Millero, F.J., 1977. The equation of state of pure water determined from sound speeds. The Journal of Chemical Physics, 66(5), pp.2142-2144. doi:10.1063/1.434179
