nematode R Documentation

Nematode Data

Description

Numbers of nematodes invading individual fly larvae for various numbers of initial challengers.

Usage

data(nematode)

Format

A data frame with 29 observations on the following 3 variables.

m

Number of challengers

r

Number of invading nematodes

y

Number of fly larvae

Source

Faddy, M. J. and Fenlon, J. S. (1999) Stochastic modelling of the invasion process of nematodes in fly larvae. Applied Statistics, 48, 31â€“37.
