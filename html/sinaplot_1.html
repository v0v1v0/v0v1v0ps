blood R Documentation

Expression data from 2095 AML/ALL and healthy bone marrow cells.

Description

Expression data from 2095 AML/ALL and healthy bone marrow cells.

Usage

data(blood)

Format

A data frame with 2095 rows and 2 columns (Class (AML/ALL subtype), Gene expression values).
