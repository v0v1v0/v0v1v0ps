Small Artificial Data set

Description

Small Toy Example Data set which was used by Sanz-Mateo et.al.

Format

The format is: int [1:13, 1:7] 10 12 17 21 9 12 12 14 13 15 ... - attr(*, "dimnames")=List of 2 ..$ : chr [1:13] "1" "2" "3" "4" ... ..$ : chr [1:7] "1" "2" "3" "4" ...
