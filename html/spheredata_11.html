The Mechanical Waves Conceptual Survey (MWCS) dataset

Description

The MWCS is the most important test to date that has been designed to evaluate studentsâ€™ understanding of four main topics in mechanical waves. It encompasses some concepts surrounding propagation, superposition, reflection, and standing waves within 22 multiple choice items.
