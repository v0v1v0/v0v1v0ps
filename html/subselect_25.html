algorithm)

initialpop)

validnovcrit(mat, criterion, H, r, p, tolval, tolsym)

prepRetOtp(output, kmin, kmax, nsol, Waldval, algorithm ,Numprb, Optimal, tl)

These functions are not to be called by the user.
