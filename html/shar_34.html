species_b R Documentation

Species b

Description

A species with positive associations to habitat 5 of landscape. Please be aware that a positive association to one habitat will inevitable lead to negative associations to other habitats (Yamada et al. 2006)

Usage

species_b

Format

A spatstat ppp object.

References

Yamada, T., Tomita, A., Itoh, A., Yamakura, T., Ohkubo, T., Kanzaki, M., Tan, S., Ashton, P.S., 2006. Habitat associations of Sterculiaceae trees in a Bornean rain forest plot. Journal of Vegetation Science 17, 559â€“566.
