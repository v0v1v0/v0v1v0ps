Synthetic data for subgxe

Description

Synthetic data for subgxe

Usage

studies

Format

A list of 5 data.frames with 12000 observations (6000 cases, 6000 controls) on 4 variables:

D

Disease status. Numeric 0-1

G

Genetic variant. Numeric 0-1

E

Exposure. Numeric 0-1

GbyE

G * E. Either 1 or 0.
