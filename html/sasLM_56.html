geoCV R Documentation

Geometric Coefficient of Variation in percentage

Description

Geometric coefficient of variation in percentage.

Usage

  geoCV(y)

Arguments

y

a numeric vector

Details

It removes NA. This is sqrt(exp(var(log(x))) - 1)*100.

Value

Geometric coefficient of variation in percentage.
