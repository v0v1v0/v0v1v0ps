fmainPos R Documentation

Calculate coordinates for title in plots

Description

Internal function called by plot.satin and quiver.

Usage

fmainPos(pu, main.pos)

Arguments

pu

Obtained by par("usr").

main.pos

Desired position, one among "topright", "topleft", "bottomright", "bottomleft").
