TCGA_survivalData R Documentation

TCGA data for survival analysis

Description

TCGA data is a clinical and microarray dataset consisting of 572 samples (observations) in row and 162 variables (20 clinical variables such as age, sex month, status,race, genotype and 142 DE genes)

Usage

data("TCGA_survivalData")
